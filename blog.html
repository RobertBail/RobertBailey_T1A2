"Thoughts on this Coder Academy course, comparison with edX/University of Adelaide" Posted: 21-03-2024 4:30PM

I decided to do this Coder Academy course at the moment, because some of the edX/University of Adelaide course was a bit rushed. It had a very good main instructor, and the classes (my class at least) were smaller, 15 people, reduced to about 10 when people dropped out. So it was a bit easier to ask questions directly to him, but some of the topics really needed 2 or 3 weeks to understand probably, instead of the one week, new topic each week. Mid last year I was deciding whether to do Coder Academy or edX/University of Adelaide or both, and by December last year, I decided I should probably do both, to familiarize with some of the topics some more, and a bit better/Australian networking and the placements. Also I have sometimes looked up some of the jobs, eg. "entry level" jobs, related to IT/coding, which often still require some experience, eg. 6 months or 2 years, so another reason I decided to do this extra 10 months of study/experience. And it looks like a few things will be covered in this course which weren't covered in edX/University of Adelaide, such as Ruby and Python.
